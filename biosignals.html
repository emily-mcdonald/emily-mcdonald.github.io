Increasing Interpersonal Understanding in Virtual Reality through Biosignal Matching

Technological innovations can make us feel both connected and isolated from other people, and technology-enabled communication has become a core part of daily life, yet sharing meaningful communication and establishing authentic connections over platforms remain a challenge. To solve this problem, we explored how we might build empathy and emotional understanding between two people through shared biosignals?
